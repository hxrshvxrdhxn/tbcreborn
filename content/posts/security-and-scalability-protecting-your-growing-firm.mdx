As companies scale, their exposure to cybersecurity risks increases significantly.

Small and mid-sized businesses often assume they are unlikely targets, but in reality they frequently lack robust security infrastructure.

Technical consulting engagements focused on **cybersecurity for small businesses** typically include:

- Security audits of current systems
- Access control reviews
- Data protection policies
- Cloud security configuration
- Incident response planning

Implementing scalable security frameworks ensures that organizations can grow without exposing sensitive data or operational systems to risk.
