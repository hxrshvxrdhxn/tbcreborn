When organizations reach approximately **50 employees**, operational complexity increases significantly. At this stage, informal technology systems often begin to break down.

A technical consulting diagnostic typically evaluates five core areas.

- *Technology Stack Alignment**
- Are tools being used across departments compatible and integrated?

- *Data Visibility**
- Can leadership access reliable, real-time operational insights?

- *Process Automation**
- Are repetitive tasks still being handled manually?

- *Security Infrastructure**
- Are systems protected against potential cybersecurity threats?

- *Scalability Readiness**
- Can the current infrastructure support future growth?

This **five-point technical diagnostic** helps mid-sized firms identify technology bottlenecks before they become serious operational constraints.
